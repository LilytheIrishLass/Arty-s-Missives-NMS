Desolate Drift II is a system discovered by Traveler "Totally_Bonkers" on an unknown date. Personal reports and field studies have shown the system to be completely desolate of any higher-functioning entities. A large number of space freighter wrecks and abandoned hulls have been reported scattered between planetary bodies. Research into these wrecks is ongoing. Popular theory claims the system to be the site of an ancient battle. Current evidence and data does not dispute this theory.
